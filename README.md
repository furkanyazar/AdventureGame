### PLACES

- Safe House
  - Attribute: Restores health
- Cave
  - Monster: Zombie (1-3)
  - Attribute: War + Loot
  - Item: Food
- Forest
  - Monster: Vampire (1-3)
  - Attribute: War + Loot
  - Item: Firewood
- River
  - Beast: Bear (1-3)
  - Attribute: War + Loot
  - Item: Water
- Shopping centre
  - Feature: Supporting items are purchased
  - Weapon: Pistol, Sword, Rifle
  - Armor: Light, Medium, Heavy
